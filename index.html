About Me

My name is Marko Markoc and I work as a system administrator at Portland State University. As a primary Storage Administrator, I am responsible for design, deployment and administration of University's Enterprise and Research Computing NAS and SAN storage environment consisting of multiple NetApp OnTAP clusters(On prem and AWS), E-Series arrays, Brocade switches and Hitachi AMS. Other responsibilities include administration of Windows and Linux servers, VMWare virtualization environment, 142 node research HPC and Commvault data protection suite. My focus is to create secure, reliable and manageable infrastructure using various automation and IaC tools like Puppet, Ansible, Packer and SCCM. I hold a bachelor's degree in IT from University of Applied Sciences Velika Gorica, Croatia and am currently pursuing a Computer Science master’s degree at Portland State University where I plan on expanding my knowledge in the areas of systems programming and high performance computing.
